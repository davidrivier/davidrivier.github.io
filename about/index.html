About David

As far as I can remember, I have always been involved with building new businesses or products, and this for a long time, well before my professional career starts (working with web agencies, start-ups or launching the first French Law portal ABCjuris.com in the late 90s).

I find nothing more exciting than the challenges associated with the creation and the development of a business and working with an extremely talented team. I help organisations understand and respond to their customers needs and translate those needs into products, designs and engineering activities to build and scale those companies.

In the last few years, my activities led me to help a number of companies with transformation and turnaround needs which requires a different type of energy and a totally different motivation. I strongly believe that in those moments, it is very important to tell it like it is to drive the agenda forward.

I am currently the chief Product Officer (CPO) at WonderBill which is parts of Shell Energy where I spent the last two years leading the product, designs, data sciences and software engineering teams to build a new type of proposition centred around household management. Building both our B2C and B2B propositions.

A short video explaining WonderBill proposition - if you are lucky you may find in this video! ;)

Previously, I was CPO and Board Member at Concrete Platform helping retail brands to improve their retail execution and store performance. I helped Concrete with its business transformation, leading the product, designs and engineering teams which included building, launching the new Concrete Platform and scaling the business several folds.
